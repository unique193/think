The Breakdown

My Top 10 favourites

In no particular order, my all time top 10 are:

  • Homeland

    • I LOVE this show. Why? Because i love the storyline; it keeps me on the edge of my seat and isn't cliched. It's definitely a must watch.

  • Power

    • James St Patrick is a spice of life. Need i say more? Haha. This show is badass; it took me by surprise.

  • Breaking Bad (obviously)

    • I jumped on the bandwagon with this one. Luckily, there were enough bandwagons to go around. It didn't dissapoint.

  • Prison Break

    • I loved the drama this show provided from the onset. Kudos to the acting too; i hated T-Bag with a passion. The sequel though? I reckon it's a flop

  • Banshee

    • I can say this is the best show that nobody knows about. It certainly surprised me and i loved every moment. I didn't know what to do with life after this one ended.

  • Lie To Me

    • Love psychology, body language, mind hacks and all that? What are you waiting for? This show is all about that! It's a perfect 10.

  • Black Mirror

    • This one is up here because I LOVE TECHNOLOGY. It's also mixed with mystery and drama... Yes please!

  • House Of Cards

    • I usually don't do politics but this show converted me. I love, love, love the drama.

  • The Mentalist

    • When this show ended.. no other show could live up to it for a good 4 months. Definitly one of my top 3 shows. It's a perfect mix of cop/ criminal action and psychology.

  • Criminal Minds

    • I just love me some dark criminal activity! It's my number 1 go to show for that. (Number 2: Law & Order: SVU because gone are the days of CSI New York)

Honourable mentions

These shows i also love and would easily recommend:

  • How To Get Away With Murder

    • Crime.. Mystery.. Law.. It's my kinda show.

  • Utopia

    • This show offers a pop of colour like no other. My favourite line was "Where is Jessica Hyde?" It's also got a really funny funky soundtrack that reminds me of farts

  • The Night Of

    • Introduced to me by my Brother.. This show totally depicts injustice. Or justice. We don't quite know yet.

  • Peaky Blinders

    • This is a show that kept me up well into the mornings. I didn't think i'd like it so much but it kicks ass.

  • The Appentice, Dragons Den (UK) & Shark Tank (Cheating but allow me ☻)

    • Gotta make that moneyyy! It's awesome to see the ideas people come up with. Plus... Drama gallore.

  • Incorporated

    • If you love technology... This show is a MUST WATCH. It's packed with it. Good jaw-dropping tech of the future.

  • The Returned

    • People coming back from the dead... the mystery behind it all? Yes please!

  • Vikings

    • Last but not least! It's the version of Game Of Thrones that actually makes sense people.

    For those of you who do trailers, check out the playlist of the shows above. Enjoy!
